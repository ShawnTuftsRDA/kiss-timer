Frequently Asked Questions

How long is the average kiss?
The average kiss lasts about 3-5 seconds. However, romantic kisses can last anywhere from 10 seconds to several minutes. Science says a kiss lasting at least 6 seconds releases bonding hormones like oxytocin.
What is the world record for longest kiss?
The Guinness World Record for longest kiss is 58 hours, 35 minutes and 58 seconds, set by Ekkachai and Laksana Tiranarat of Thailand in 2013. We don't recommend trying to beat it.
Does kissing have health benefits?
Yes! Kissing can reduce stress, lower blood pressure, boost immunity, and release feel-good hormones like dopamine, serotonin, and oxytocin. It also burns a few calories per minute.
Can I use the timer feature?
Absolutely! After generating a kiss duration, tap "Start Timer" and it will count down your exact kiss length. The bar fills up as time passes. When it hits zero — you're done!
